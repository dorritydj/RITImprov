Joe Pisanzio
Hometown: Penfield, NY<br/>
(BrainWreck Improv, 2010-2012)<br/><br/>
He started doing Improv in high school, where he lead the troupe Malarkey Jargon. From high school he took a hiatus from improv for a year or two until he joined Brainwreck Improv at RIT. He is also a member of the Village Idiots. In the past year Joe has been "bit by the improv bug" and has developed a deep passion for it. Joe also likes to jump on things.
