Brittany Ransbottom
(Improvessionals 2012 - 2012)<br/><br/>
Raised by otters Brittany is the third fastest swimmer in river water in the world. She has been nominated for two Nobel Peace prizes, but due to sibling rivlery, Bono has made sure she never won. Since the last world war, Brittany has devoted her life to helping orphans in the Sudan, and hurting orphans in Ireland. her favorite color is blue and she adores cobwebs.
